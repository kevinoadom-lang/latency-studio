Privacy Policy

Your privacy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Latency ("we", "us") is an AI modelling and e-commerce imagery studio. This policy explains what we collect when you visit uselatency.com or get in touch, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We've kept it plain — no fine print.

What we collect

Two kinds of information:

  • What you give us. When you join the waitlist or send an enquiry, we collect your name, brand, the brief you describe, and your email address.
  • What's collected automatically. When you browse the site, analytics tools may record pages viewed, approximate location (from your IP address), and basic device and browser details. This only happens if you've allowed it — see "Cookies and consent" below.

The tools we use

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run the site and understand how it's used:

  • Google Analytics 4 — measures site traffic and which pages people visit.
  • Meta Pixel — measures the performance of our ads on Facebook and Instagram.
  • Meta Conversions API — when you submit the waitlist form, a copy of the event (including a securely hashed version of your email) is sent to Meta from our server to attribute the enquiry to an ad.
  • Formspree — delivers your waitlist enquiry to our inbox.
  • Netlify — hosts the website.

Cookies and consent

Analytics and advertising cookies are switched off by default. Using Google Consent Mode, both Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel start in a "denied" state and only begin storing data if you opt in. Your choice is remembered in your browser (a value stored locally under the key ll_consent) so you aren't asked every visit.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's site data for uselatency.com, or by blocking cookies in your browser settings. You can also opt out directly through Google Analytics' opt-out and your Meta ad preferences.

Why we collect it

  • To respond to your enquiry and discuss a possible booking.
  • To understand how the site is used and improve it.
  • To measure and improve our advertising.

Who we share it with

We don't sell your information. We only share it with the providers named above, and only so they can perform their function for us. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, or opt out of analytics and advertising at any time. Just email us and we'll take care of it.

Contact

Questions, or want your data removed? Email hello@uselatency.com.

Changes to this policy

If we update how we handle data, we'll revise this page and change the "Last updated" date above.
